## One-liner
BaseRank is a prediction market mini app inside the Base app for Base ecosystem outcomes —
the first app to let users predict Base app leaderboard rankings and onchain metrics.

## What we've built
- Live prediction market mini app at baserank-miniapp.vercel.app
- Audited smart contract stack (EventRegistry + TierMarket + BatchClaimer) on Base
- 149/149 passing tests, two independent audits, no critical findings
- Weekly prediction markets around the Base app leaderboard

## The gap we fill
No one is running Base-app-economy prediction markets inside the Base app.

Kalshi runs general-purpose markets. Polymarket runs general-purpose markets.
Neither runs:
- "Will Aerodrome stay #1 by fees this week?"
- "Will app X enter the Top 5 by next snapshot?"
- "Which app gains the most weekly active addresses?"

These are the markets Base ecosystem participants actually want.

## The ask
1. Access to the Builders Program ($2M Base co-sponsored grants)
2. A custom `BASERANK-` market series on Kalshi for Base app economy outcomes
3. Engineering support to wire BaseRank UI to Kalshi settlement layer
